About Us

Welcome to Il-Quran Imqaddes bil-Malti, the Maltese translation of the Holy Quran. Our mission is to provide Maltese-speaking Muslims with an authentic and accessible translation of the Quran, helping them understand its teachings and live a spiritual life.

The Translators

This translation was undertaken by Dr. Muhammad S. Khatib and Dr. Ali F. Dalli, renowned scholars in the field of Islamic studies. Their work reflects a deep understanding of the Quran and the Maltese language.

Our Purpose

We believe in making the Quran's wisdom available to all, no matter their language or background. Our aim is to empower the Maltese-speaking Muslim community with knowledge, helping them to strengthen their faith and understand the teachings of Islam.

If you would like to learn more or get involved, please contact us. We welcome your feedback and support.
